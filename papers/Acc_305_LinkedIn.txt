Wave 7 adds the staircase_lift rule. The task (4c3d4a41) presents an 8x20 grid
split into two halves: a staircase on the left and four color bars on the right.
The staircase column heights directly encode where each color bar must end in the
output. The formula: MaxEnd = 5 - H, where H is the staircase height for that bar.

No neural network. No pretraining. One glass-box named rule, verified against every
training pair before the benchmark runs.
