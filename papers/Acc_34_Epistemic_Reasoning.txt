ABSTRACT

We report Rung 34 of Mentova's 48-rung reasoning ladder: epistemic reasoning.
Mentova reasons about what agents know, believe, and are ignorant of.
Alice knows canary_is_yellow with confidence 0.9. Bob is ignorant of
tweety_is_canary (no knowledge or belief recorded). Fire_is_hot is not common
knowledge (not all agents have it in their knowledge base).
Pass criterion met: knowledge, belief, and ignorance correctly distinguished.


INTRODUCTION

Epistemic logic (Hintikka, 1962) formalises knowledge and belief. Rung 34
gives Mentova an explicit knowledge base per agent with confidence levels,
and predicates to check knowledge, belief, ignorance, and common knowledge.


BACKGROUND

Multi-agent epistemic reasoning is foundational to game theory, planning under
uncertainty, and natural language understanding of mental state verbs.


METHOD

A new module, src/mentova/epistemic.pl, implements:

    mentova_epistemic(+Query, -Result, -Justification)

Three agents: mentor (4 knowledge facts), alice (3), bob (2).
Queries: knows, believes, ignorant, common_knowledge, what_does.


DEMONSTRATION

    % Query Mentova for epistemic reasoning about 'knows(alice'.
    ?- mentova_query(epistemic, knows(alice, canary_is_yellow), R).
    R = answer(knows(alice, canary_is_yellow, 0.9), ...)

    % Query Mentova for epistemic reasoning about 'ignorant(bob'.
    ?- mentova_query(epistemic, ignorant(bob, tweety_is_canary), R).
    R = answer(ignorant(bob, tweety_is_canary), ...)


RESULTS

Alice knows canary_is_yellow at 0.9. Bob is ignorant of tweety_is_canary.
Fire_is_hot is not common knowledge. Pass criterion met.


SIGNIFICANCE

Epistemic reasoning enables Mentova to model other agents' mental states,
support theory-of-mind reasoning, and distinguish what is known from what
is assumed.


HONEST LIMITATIONS

Knowledge and belief are stored facts, not derived from evidence or
observation. A full epistemic agent would update knowledge from perception.


REPRODUCIBILITY

Requirements: SWI-Prolog 9.0.4 or later.

  git clone https://github.com/ai-university-aiu/Mentova.git
  cd Mentova
  % Load the Mentova bootstrap into SWI-Prolog, execute the query, and halt.
  swipl -l src/mentova/mentova.pl \
        % Supply a goal or termination instruction to the SWI-Prolog command.
        -g "mentova_boot, mentova_query(epistemic, knows(alice, canary_is_yellow), R), write(R), nl" \
        % Supply a goal or termination instruction to the SWI-Prolog command.
        -t halt
