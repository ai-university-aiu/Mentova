ABSTRACT

We report Rung 41 of Mentova's 48-rung reasoning ladder: narrative reasoning.
Mentova understands story structure: setup, conflict, resolution, character roles,
causal chains, and lessons. For "The Tortoise and the Hare," the arc is
setup=hare_boasts, conflict=race_begins, resolution=tortoise_wins. The tortoise's
role is hero; the hare's role is foil. Pass criterion met: narrative arc and
character roles returned from story identifier.


INTRODUCTION

Narrative reasoning concerns comprehension of story structure, plot causality,
and character roles. It is central to natural language understanding and the
cognitive basis of human reasoning.


BACKGROUND

Narrative intelligence (Schank and Abelson, 1977) uses scripts and story grammars
to model sequential event comprehension. Propp's morphology (1928) identifies
canonical character roles (hero, antagonist, helper) and narrative stages.


METHOD

A new module, src/mentova/narrative.pl, implements:

    mentova_narrative(+Query, -Result, -Justification)

Three stories: tortoise_hare, boy_wolf, cinderella. Each with events, roles,
arc, and lesson. Queries: arc, characters, role, causal_chain, lesson, what_stories.


DEMONSTRATION

    % Query Mentova for narrative reasoning about 'arc(tortoise_hare)'.
    ?- mentova_query(narrative, arc(tortoise_hare), R).
    R = answer(arc(hare_boasts, race_begins, tortoise_wins), ...)

    % Query Mentova for narrative reasoning about 'lesson(boy_wolf)'.
    ?- mentova_query(narrative, lesson(boy_wolf), R).
    R = answer(lesson(liars_not_believed), ...)

    % Query Mentova for narrative reasoning about 'role(cinderella'.
    ?- mentova_query(narrative, role(cinderella, cinderella), R).
    R = answer(role(cinderella, hero), ...)


RESULTS

Arcs, lessons, roles correctly retrieved for all three stories.
Pass criterion met.


SIGNIFICANCE

Narrative reasoning is foundational to reading comprehension, story generation,
summarization, and the cultural transmission of knowledge through fables and myths.


HONEST LIMITATIONS

Stories are manually encoded. Narrative understanding from free text requires
natural language processing beyond this symbolic representation.


REPRODUCIBILITY

Requirements: SWI-Prolog 9.0.4 or later.
