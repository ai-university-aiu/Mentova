Accomplishment 233: Systematic Generation of Scene Transformation Rule Candidates (transformgen Pack, tg_*, Layer 192)

Author: D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU, Creator and Owner of PrologAI and Mentova,
ORCID 0009-0001-9246-5758, linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/


Abstract

PrologAI gains its 213th work package: the transformgen pack, a suite of 14
tg_* predicates that systematically generate sets of candidate rule terms from
scene data or training pairs. Where heuristic inference guesses the most likely
rules, transformgen exhaustively enumerates every plausible rule within a
bounded search space.


Motivation

The gridsolve and ruleinfer packs use pattern-matching heuristics to infer the
most likely transformation rule from training examples. These heuristics work
well for common patterns. But ARC-AGI-2 tasks include unusual rules that
heuristics miss. For those tasks, the only reliable strategy is to generate
ALL plausible rules from the scene data and filter to those consistent with
every training pair.


Generators

tg_recolor_candidates: all recolor(Old,New) rules for distinct color pairs in
the scene. If the scene has colors {r, b, g}, this generates 6 recolor rules.

tg_recolor_all_candidates, tg_remove_candidates, tg_keep_candidates: one rule
per color, systematically covering the filter and uniform-recolor spaces.

tg_shift_candidates: all shift(DR,DC) within bounds. For MaxDR=MaxDC=3, this
generates 48 shift rules covering all directions and distances up to 3 steps.

tg_reflect_candidates: reflect_h and reflect_v for given grid dimensions.

tg_top_candidates: top_n(K) for K in 1 to min(MaxN, scene size).

tg_color_map_candidate: derive a color_map rule from one Before-After pair
by positional matching, extracting changed-color pairs.


Integration Predicates

tg_from_scenes takes a single Before-After pair and returns the union of all
applicable generator predicates. tg_from_pairs does the same for a list of
training pairs. tg_filter_consistent filters any rule list to those fully
consistent with every training pair, using a self-contained rule application
engine. tg_all_scene_candidates generates all candidates from a single scene
with a given shift bound.


Test Coverage

42 of 42 unit tests pass. Tests cover all 14 predicates: color extraction,
all generator predicates, color_map derivation, from_scenes and from_pairs
integration, filter_consistent for all major rule types, and count/bound
operations.


Significance

The transformgen pack closes the enumeration gap in the ARC-AGI-2 reasoning
stack. Combined with gridsolve (for heuristic inference), scenerank (for
coverage ranking), and tg_filter_consistent (for exhaustive filter-based
selection), PrologAI now has both fast heuristic and complete exhaustive
paths for finding the correct scene transformation rule.

This is WP 213, Mentova's 233rd accomplishment.
