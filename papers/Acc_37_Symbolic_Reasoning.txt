ABSTRACT

We report Rung 37 of Mentova's 48-rung reasoning ladder: symbolic reasoning.
Mentova simplifies algebraic expressions by matching identity rules. add(x, 0)
simplifies to x (additive identity), mul(y, 1) simplifies to y (multiplicative
identity), neg(neg(z)) simplifies to z (double negation). Each simplification
names the applied rule. Pass criterion met: expression simplified with identity
rule named.


INTRODUCTION

Symbolic reasoning involves manipulating formal expressions according to rules —
the foundation of algebra, logic, and computer algebra systems. Rung 37 equips
Mentova with algebraic identity rules and expression simplification.


BACKGROUND

Symbolic AI (Newell and Simon, 1976) manipulates symbolic structures by rule
application. Computer algebra systems (Macsyma, Mathematica) automate this for
mathematics.


METHOD

A new module, src/mentova/symbolic.pl, implements:

    mentova_symbolic(+Query, -Result, -Justification)

Ten identity rules: additive_identity, multiplicative_identity, zero_product,
double_negation, distributive, commutativity_add, idempotent_or/and,
absorption_and, de_morgan_not_and. Queries: simplify, identify, substitute, match.


DEMONSTRATION

    ?- mentova_query(symbolic, simplify(add(x, 0)), R).
    R = answer(simplified(add(x,0), x, additive_identity),
               just(symbolic(simplification(add(x,0)), rule(additive_identity), result(x))))

    ?- mentova_query(symbolic, simplify(neg(neg(z))), R).
    R = answer(simplified(neg(neg(z)), z, double_negation), ...)


RESULTS

Three simplifications demonstrated. Each names the applied rule.
Pass criterion met.


SIGNIFICANCE

Symbolic reasoning underpins computer algebra, theorem proving, program analysis,
and formal verification. It is the original foundation of AI.


HONEST LIMITATIONS

Simplification is single-step; it does not recursively simplify sub-expressions.
Full algebraic simplification would require repeated application with a normal-form
check.


REPRODUCIBILITY

Requirements: SWI-Prolog 9.0.4 or later.

  git clone https://github.com/ai-university-aiu/Mentova.git
  cd Mentova
  swipl -l src/mentova/mentova.pl \
        -g "mentova_boot, mentova_query(symbolic, simplify(neg(neg(z))), R), write(R), nl" \
        -t halt
