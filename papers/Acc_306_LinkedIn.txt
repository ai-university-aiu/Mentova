PrologAI just solved its 8th ARC-AGI-2 task.

Wave 8 adds the bar_sort rule.  The task (31f7f899) shows a grid with a
horizontal divider row and several vertical color bars that cross it.  Each
bar has a height above the divider and a height below.  The rule: sort the
bars left-to-right by their above-height, shortest first, reassigning colors
to bar slots accordingly.

No neural network.  No pretraining.  One glass-box named rule, verified
against every training pair before the benchmark runs.

Running total: 8 / 120 = 6.67%.
