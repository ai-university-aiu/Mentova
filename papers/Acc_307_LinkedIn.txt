PrologAI just solved its 9th ARC-AGI-2 task.

Wave 9 adds the panel_overlay rule. The task (7491f3cf) shows a grid split into four
5x5 panels. Panel 1 contains a dividing wall (5 cells, 8-connected) plus one isolated
seed cell. The rule: flood-fill (4-connected) from the seed, treating the wall as
blocked. Cells reachable from the seed take values from panel 2; unreachable cells
take values from panel 3; wall cells prefer panel 2, fall back to panel 3.

The seed is always isolated from the wall (no 8-neighbor in common), pointing the
algorithm to the correct side. The wall shapes across the four training pairs include
an anti-diagonal, a vertical line, a main diagonal, and a horizontal bar. The test
uses an L-shape. One rule handles all of them.

No neural network. No pretraining. One glass-box named rule, verified against every
training pair before the benchmark runs.

Running total: 9 / 120 = 7.50%.
