Before searching for a rule, classify the task. That one step reduces the search space dramatically.

Work Package 273 introduces the taskcat pack: 14 tc_* predicates implementing the Chain of Responsibility solving pattern. tc_categorize classifies a task as single_rule, multi_step, context_gated, or symbol_table. tc_suggest_strategies returns an ordered fallback list. tc_confidence scores how certain we are.

273 work packages. Systematic reasoning, not brute force.
