MENTOVA ACC_76: THE GRID PACK - VISUAL PERCEPTION FOR ARC-AGI

Mentova can now see.

Not in a metaphorical sense. In the literal, glass-box, every-step-auditable sense.

The grid pack (PR 56, Layer 35 of the PrologAI cognitive architecture) gives Mentova native perception of rectangular color grids - the visual substrate of the ARC-AGI benchmark.

ARC-AGI is the benchmark designed by Francois Chollet to test genuine fluid intelligence. Each puzzle shows a few input-output grid pairs. The solver must find the transformation rule and apply it to a new input. ARC-AGI-2 (2025) raises the bar to compositional generalization.

Before Mentova can reason about a transformation, it must perceive the grids.

The grid pack provides that perception. 26 predicates. All pure Prolog. All glass-box.

What Mentova can now do:

gd_size/3 - query grid dimensions.

gd_cell/4, gd_row/3, gd_col/3 - access cells, rows, columns by zero-based index.

gd_colors/2, gd_color_count/3, gd_color_map/3 - analyze and transform colors.

gd_objects/3, gd_connected/3, gd_bounding_box/3 - extract 4-connected objects and find their bounding boxes.

gd_rotate90/2, gd_rotate180/2, gd_rotate270/2 - rotate grids clockwise.

gd_reflect_h/2, gd_reflect_v/2, gd_reflect_d1/2, gd_reflect_d2/2 - reflect across all four axes of the D4 dihedral group (the symmetry group of the square).

gd_translate/5, gd_crop/6, gd_overlay/5 - move, extract, and composite grids.

gd_diff/3, gd_equal/2 - compare grids and find exactly what changed.

gd_symmetry/2 - detect which symmetry axes a grid has.

gd_fill/5, gd_make/4, gd_set_cell/5 - construct and mutate grids.

31 PLUnit tests. All pass.

This is not a neural feature extractor. There is no black box here. Every operation is a named Prolog predicate that can be called, inspected, traced, and reasoned about.

The grid pack is the perceptual foundation. The next step is inference.
