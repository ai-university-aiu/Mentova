Mentova Accomplishment 166: The Cell-Wise Grid Arithmetic Pack

PrologAI gains its 146th work package today.

The gridmath pack (Layer 125, gm_* prefix) provides 14 predicates for
applying arithmetic operations to two-dimensional grids cell by cell.

These predicates extend the numseq pack (Layer 124, nr_*) into two
dimensions. Where numseq operates on 1D lists, gridmath operates on 2D
grids.

The 14 predicates cover:

Five cell-wise binary operations on two same-sized grids: addition,
subtraction, multiplication, maximum, and minimum (gm_add, gm_sub,
gm_mul, gm_max, gm_min).

Three scalar transforms: multiply every cell by K, add D to every cell,
take the absolute value of every cell (gm_scale, gm_offset, gm_abs).

Two comparison masks: a 1/0 mask where 1 marks equal cells, and its
complement where 1 marks differing cells (gm_eq_mask, gm_diff_mask).

Two value constraints: clamp to a range [Lo, Hi], and modulo M
(gm_clamp, gm_modulo).

Two aggregates: sum of all cells, product of all cells
(gm_sum, gm_product).
