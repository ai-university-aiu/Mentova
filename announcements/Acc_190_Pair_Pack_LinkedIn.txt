Accomplishment 190: Object Pairing and D4-Canonical Shape Correspondence

PrologAI now has 170 work packages.

The pair pack (pr_*, Layer 149) provides 14 predicates for grouping, comparing,
and matching obj(Color, Cells) terms by color, size, and D4-canonical shape.

The key capability: a horizontal two-cell pair and a vertical two-cell pair are
identified as the same shape. An L-shape and its 90-degree rotation are the same
shape. Any two objects related by any combination of rotations and reflections
(the 8 elements of the dihedral group D4) are the same shape.

This is done by: normalizing each shape to the origin, applying all 8 D4
symmetric transforms, re-normalizing each result, and taking the
lexicographically smallest one as the canonical form.

The pack gives you:
• Property access: pr_obj_shape/2, pr_obj_color/2, pr_obj_size/2
• Pairwise tests: pr_shape_eq/2, pr_color_eq/2, pr_size_eq/2
• Grouping: pr_group_shape/2, pr_group_color/2, pr_group_size/2
• Unique object: pr_unique_color/2, pr_unique_size/2
• Cross-scene matching: pr_match_shape/3, pr_match_color/3, pr_match_size/3
