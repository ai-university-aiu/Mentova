Mentova Accomplishment 213: Object Pair Relation Analysis (Objrel Pack, or_*, Layer 172)

PrologAI now includes an objrel pack (or_*, Layer 172) - 14 predicates for
computing pairwise relationships between two obj(Color, Cells) terms.

Colors are ignored in every predicate. All reasoning uses cell positions only.

The pack provides:

- or_overlap: do the two objects share at least one cell?
- or_shared_cells: which cells appear in both objects?
- or_n_shared: how many shared cells?
- or_touch4: are they 4-adjacent (Manhattan distance 1) without overlapping?
- or_touch8: are they 8-adjacent (Chebyshev 1, includes diagonal) without overlapping?
- or_contains: does Obj1's cell set include all of Obj2's cells?
- or_union_cells: sorted union of both cell sets
- or_dist: Euclidean distance between centroids (float)
- or_manhattan: Manhattan distance between centroids
- or_direction: compass direction from Obj1 to Obj2 (n/s/e/w/ne/nw/se/sw/same)
- or_aligned_h: do both objects have the same centroid row?
- or_aligned_v: do both objects have the same centroid column?
- or_gap_rows: how many empty rows lie between the two bounding boxes?
- or_gap_cols: how many empty columns lie between the two bounding boxes?
