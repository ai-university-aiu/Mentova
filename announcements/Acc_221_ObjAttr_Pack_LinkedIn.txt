PrologAI now has an objattr pack - 14 oa_* predicates for computing aggregate statistics
and rankings over a list of obj(Color, Cells) terms.

The pack answers scene-level questions that previous packs left to calling code: Which
color has the most total cells? Which object is largest? Are all objects the same shape?
What is the most common size?

It covers total cell count, per-color object and cell counts, dominant and rarest colors,
unique-color detection (backtrackable), size ranking, position ranking, majority size mode,
and three uniformity tests - all-same color, all-same size, and all-same form. The form
test normalizes each object to the origin and compares sorted cell lists, so two objects
at different positions and in different colors match if their relative cell arrangements
are identical.

41 of 41 unit tests pass.

This is WP 201, Mentova's 221st accomplishment.
