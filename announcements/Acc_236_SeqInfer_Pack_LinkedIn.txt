PrologAI now has a seqinfer pack - 14 sq_* predicates for finding multi-step
transformation rules from training pairs.

Single-rule search finds: "recolor r to b explains all training pairs."

seqinfer finds: "first recolor r to b, then shift everything down 1 row."

Key predicates:
- sq_apply: apply a list of rules in sequence (13 rule types supported)
- sq_infer_2step: find the best 2-step rule sequence
- sq_infer_3step: find the best 3-step rule sequence
- sq_solve: combined solver (tries 2-step first, falls back to 1-step)
- sq_consistent: verify a sequence explains all training pairs
- sq_coverage: count training pairs explained by a sequence
- sq_default_rules: 30 standard single-step candidates

44 of 44 unit tests pass.
