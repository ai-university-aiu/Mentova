Mentova just gained its 94th work package: the sym pack.

14 sy_* predicates covering the full D4 dihedral group and symmetry detection:

  sy_reflect_h    - left-right mirror
  sy_reflect_v    - top-bottom mirror
  sy_transpose    - main diagonal reflection
  sy_rotate90     - 90 degrees clockwise
  sy_rotate180    - 180 degrees
  sy_rotate270    - 270 CW (= 90 CCW)
  sy_has_h_symm   - does this grid look the same flipped left-right?
  sy_has_v_symm   - does it look the same flipped top-bottom?
  sy_has_rot2_symm - does it look the same rotated 180?
  sy_has_rot4_symm - does it look the same rotated 90?
  sy_symmetries   - list all symmetries present
  sy_make_h_symm  - mirror left half to right
  sy_make_v_symm  - mirror top half to bottom
  sy_d4_orbit     - all 8 D4 variants, deduplicated

Rotations are composites: rotate90 = transpose + reflect_h.

43/43 acceptance tests pass. General-purpose naming. Pure Prolog. Glass-box.
