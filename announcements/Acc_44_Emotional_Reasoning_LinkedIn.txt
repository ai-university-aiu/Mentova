MENTOVA APPRAISES EMOTIONAL SIGNIFICANCE — Rung 44 Accomplished

Author: D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU (Artificial Intelligence University). Creator of PrologAI and Mentova. Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCID): 0009-0001-9246-5758. (https://www.linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/)


I am pleased to announce the accomplishment of Rung 44 of the Mentova reasoning ladder: Emotional Reasoning.

Mentova can now perform cognitive appraisal: primary appraisal (is this event desirable or threatening?) and secondary appraisal (can I handle it?) — predicting the resulting emotion, its intensity, and the action tendency it generates.

Demonstrated live:

    mentova_query(emotional, appraise(alice, win_competition), R)
    → emotion(joy, high, approach)

    mentova_query(emotional, how_might_feel(bob, exam_tomorrow), R)
    → likely_emotion(anxiety, moderate, prepare)

    mentova_query(emotional, appraise(alice, lose_contest), R)
    → emotion(sadness, moderate, withdraw)

Mentova does not simulate feelings. It reasons about them — and shows the appraisal chain that leads to each emotional prediction.
