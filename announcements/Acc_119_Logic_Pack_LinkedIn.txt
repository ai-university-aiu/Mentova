Accomplishment 119: The Logic Pack - Boolean and Mask Grid Operations

Mentova's 97th work package is live.

The logic pack adds 14 lg_* predicates for treating grids as boolean masks:
AND, OR, XOR, NOT, diff, overlay, mask creation and application, row and
column presence flags, and cell-wise equality grids.

With the logic pack, Mentova can now combine two grids as sets - finding
where they overlap, where they differ, and where exactly one of them has
content. These are the boolean primitives that visual rule learning needs
when the answer is a combination of two input grids.

All 42 acceptance tests pass. The pack is documented in SPARC Specification
v64 (Part 89, LG-LG-001 through LG-LG-014), Refinement v112, and the
tutorial Chapter 60.
