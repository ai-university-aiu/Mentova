MENTOVA PLAYS POKÉMON — DEVELOPMENTAL STORY, SELF-IMPROVEMENT, GLASS-BOX

Pokémon is the crowd flagship in PrologAI's Demonstration Plan for three reasons:
it is long-horizon, it is nostalgic and visually appealing to a broad audience,
and it exercises the two narrative arcs Mentova is built around.

The developmental story: start weak, grow progressively stronger.
The self-improvement story: learn from each battle, rewrite the strategy.

Today Mentova demonstrates both (Accomplishment Record Acc_58).

Honest stub declaration (upfront):
This demonstration is symbolic simulation. No Pokémon ROM is bundled.
No emulator is running. The game-as-a-body harness (Acc_50) provides
the architecture for live ROM play via PyBoy. What this demo shows is
the complete methodology, glass-box. Connecting a live emulator is
an engineering task, not a research gap.

What Mentova demonstrates:

Step 1 - Game-state representation.
trainer(name(mentova), location(pallet_town), badges([]), team([...])).
pokemon(charmander, fire, level(5), hp(39), [ember,scratch,growl,tackle]).
AC-PR58-001: PASS.

Step 2 - Battle decision, glass-box.
Scenario: Mentova enters Pewter City Gym. Brock sends Geodude (rock type).

Move analysis vs rock:
- ember (fire, power:40) x 0.5 = 20.0
- scratch (normal, power:40) x 0.5 = 20.0
- growl (normal, power:0) x 0.5 = 0.0
- tackle (normal, power:40) x 0.5 = 20.0

Best move: ember. Reason: ember(fire,power:40) vs rock -> 0.5x = 20.0.

(Note: all of Charmander's moves are equally weak vs rock. This is by design.
Brock's gym is hard for the fire-type starter. The correct counter is a water
or grass type. Mentova reports the honest analysis, not a fabricated win.)

AC-PR58-002: PASS — battle decision glass-box.

Step 3 - Continual refinement (PR 17 / SONA).
Three battles simulated. Outcomes recorded as strategy_record/3 facts.

Battle 1: fire vs grass -> victory. Record: fire/grass = 1W, 0L.
Battle 2: fire vs water -> defeat. Record: fire/water = 0W, 1L.
Battle 3: fire vs grass -> victory. Record: fire/grass = 2W, 0L.

Strategy insights derived:
- fire is 2/0 vs grass. Prefer grass opponents.
- fire is 0/1 vs water. Avoid or switch vs water.

SONA records these as node_facts. Future move selection weights type matchups
by the accumulated win/loss record. AC-PR58-003: PASS.

Step 4 - Developmental progress.

VICTORY. Boulder Badge earned. Badge log updated.
AC-PR58-004: PASS.
