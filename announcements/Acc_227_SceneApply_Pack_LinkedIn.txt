PrologAI now has a sceneapply pack - 14 sa_* predicates that apply symbolic
rule terms to obj(Color, Cells) scene lists.

The rule vocabulary covers every scene-level transformation: recolor(Old,New),
recolor_all(New), color_map(Map), shift(DR,DC), to_origin, reflect_h/v,
remove_color, keep_color, sort_size_desc/asc, sort_pos, top_n(N), dedup_form.

The core predicate sa_apply dispatches on the rule term structure to the
appropriate helper. sa_apply_seq applies a sequence of rules in order - the
first symbolic pipeline for scene transformations in PrologAI.

sa_verify, sa_verify_seq, sa_verify_all, and sa_verify_seq_all confirm that
a rule or sequence of rules correctly transforms training examples. Once
verified, sa_apply produces the output.

Combined with ruleinfer (which discovers rules from examples), sceneapply
completes the learn-and-apply cycle: infer the rule, verify it across all pairs,
apply it to the test input.
