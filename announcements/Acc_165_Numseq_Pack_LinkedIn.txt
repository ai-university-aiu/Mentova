Mentova Accomplishment 165: The Numerical Sequence Operations Pack

PrologAI gains its 145th work package today.

The numseq pack (Layer 124, nr_* prefix) provides 14 predicates for
analyzing and transforming one-dimensional lists of numbers.

These predicates pair naturally with the cross pack (Layer 121), which
extracts rows, columns, and diagonals from two-dimensional grids.

Together they form a pipeline: extract a slice, then reason about its
numerical content.

The 14 predicates cover:

Pairwise differences (nr_diff) and cumulative sums (nr_cumsum).

Running maximum (nr_running_max) and running minimum (nr_running_min).

Five element-wise binary operations: addition, subtraction,
multiplication, maximum, and minimum (nr_zip_add, nr_zip_sub,
nr_zip_mul, nr_zip_max, nr_zip_min).

Scalar multiply (nr_scale) and scalar offset (nr_offset).

Non-decreasing test (nr_is_sorted) and arithmetic sequence test
(nr_is_arith).

And shortest repeating period detection (nr_period).

All 42 acceptance tests pass.

Architecture v104, Refinement v158, Completion v161.
