PrologAI just crossed 68 work packages.

The new crop pack gives Mentova a complete vocabulary for rectangular subgrid operations.

cr_bbox - find the tight bounding box of all non-background content.

cr_crop_bbox - extract any rectangular subgrid by inclusive row/col bounds.

cr_crop_content - crop a grid to its content in one step (no background borders).

cr_pad - add a uniform N-cell color border on all four sides.

cr_strip_border - remove N rows/cols from every side. Inverse of cr_pad.

cr_split_h and cr_split_v - split a grid horizontally or vertically at a given row or column.

cr_rows and cr_cols - extract any contiguous row or column band.

cr_stitch_h and cr_stitch_v - join two grids side by side or top to bottom. Inverses of the split predicates.

cr_embed - place a subgrid at any (row, col) position inside a base grid.

cr_center - extract the centered N x M subgrid from a larger grid.

cr_quadrants - split into four quadrants at the midpoint row and column.

This matters because:

Most visual reasoning tasks involve boundaries. Where does the content start? Which region matters? How do we extract it cleanly?

cr_crop_content strips background borders in one predicate call. cr_quadrants decomposes four-fold structure in one call. cr_embed assembles the output grid from transformed parts.
