Accomplishment 101: Mentova can now apply gravity to grids.

The gravity pack (gv_* predicates, Layer 60) gives Mentova 14 new tools for directional settling:

- gv_fall_down/3: non-background cells sink to the bottom of their column
- gv_fall_up/3: non-background cells rise to the top
- gv_fall_left/3, gv_fall_right/3: non-background cells shift to the ends of their row
- gv_settle_color/4: a specific color falls to the bottom past background cells, while other colors act as obstacles
- gv_float_color/4: a specific color rises to the top
- gv_apply_col/4, gv_apply_row/4: custom column and row transforms

Combined with the induction pack added last session, Mentova can now form and test hypotheses like "the output is the input after color X fell to the bottom."
