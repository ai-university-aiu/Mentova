Ask Mentova what a bicycle is made of:

    Query: parts(bicycle)
    Answer: [wheel-rolls, frame-supports, pedal-drives, chain-transmits, brake-stops, handlebar-steers]
    Justification: system_parts(bicycle, [...])

Then ask what happens when the chain fails:

    Query: if_fails(bicycle, chain)
    Answer: drivetrain stops, pedaling has no effect, bicycle cannot move under power
    Justification: chain role is transmit, failure cascades to pedal-drives, motion lost

And ask what behavior emerges from all those parts working together:

    Query: behavior(bicycle)
    Answer: enables human-powered locomotion via mechanical force transmission
    Justification: wheel(rolls) + pedal(drives) + chain(transmits) + frame(supports) → locomotion
