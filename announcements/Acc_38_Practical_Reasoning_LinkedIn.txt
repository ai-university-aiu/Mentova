MENTOVA CHOOSES THE RIGHT ACTION FOR THE RIGHT GOAL — Rung 38 Accomplished

Author: D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU (Artificial Intelligence University). Creator and Owner of PrologAI and Mentova. Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCID): 0009-0001-9246-5758. (https://www.linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/)


I am pleased to announce the accomplishment of Rung 38 of the Mentova reasoning ladder: Practical Reasoning.

Mentova can now select the best available action for any registered agent goal, weighing feasibility and cost.

Demonstrated live:

    mentova_query(practical, best_action(alice, learn_programming), R)
    → action(enrol_in_course, feasibility(0.9), cost(low), towards(learn_programming))

    mentova_query(practical, goal_plan(alice, get_fit), R)
    → plan(alice, get_fit, actions([join_gym, exercise_regularly, eat_healthy]))

    mentova_query(practical, why_do(alice, enrol_in_course), R)
    → because(alice, enrol_in_course, serves_goal(learn_programming))

Every decision traces to the goal it serves and the feasibility/cost weighting — fully auditable practical deliberation.
