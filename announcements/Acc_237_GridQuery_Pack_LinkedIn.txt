PrologAI now has a gridquery pack - 14 gq_* predicates for querying raw grids
(list-of-lists format) without converting to scene objects.

Key predicates:
- gq_size: height and width
- gq_at: color at position (R,C)
- gq_row / gq_col: extract a row or column
- gq_colors: all distinct colors in the grid
- gq_n_cells: count cells of a specific color
- gq_most_freq_color: most common color (usually the background)
- gq_region: extract a rectangular sub-grid
- gq_diff: list of positions where two grids differ
- gq_n_diff: count of differing cells
- gq_transpose: swap rows and columns
- gq_replace: change one cell, return a new grid
- gq_fill_region: fill a rectangle with a color

45 of 45 unit tests pass.
