Acc_283: PrologAI gains connected component analysis, flood fill, and object manipulation.

The gridobj pack (Layer 242, gob_* prefix) adds 14 predicates for treating symbolic
grids as collections of discrete objects rather than flat color arrays.

4-connected BFS discovers which cells belong to each object. From there: query color,
size, and bounding box; generate a mask or extract the bounding-box subgrid; flood fill
a region with a new color; fill enclosed Bg regions surrounded by non-Bg cells; remove
an object; or move it to a new position.

The enclosed-fill predicate uses a reverse BFS strategy: seed simultaneously from all
border Bg cells, collect everything reachable, then fill everything that is NOT
reachable - the enclosed regions.
