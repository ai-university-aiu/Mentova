PrologAI now has a gridnbr pack - 14 gn_* predicates for analyzing cell
neighborhoods and applying morphological operations on raw grids.

What it does:

- gn_nbr4 / gn_nbr8: list values at 4- or 8-connected neighbors (omits out-of-bounds)
- gn_count4 / gn_count8: count a given color among neighbors
- gn_count4_grid / gn_count8_grid: produce a full count grid for every cell
- gn_any4: does any 4-neighbor have this color? (deterministic)
- gn_all4: do ALL 4-neighbors have this color?
- gn_mark_border: label foreground cells that touch the background as border
- gn_mark_isolated: label non-background cells with no same-color 4-neighbor as isolated
- gn_expand_color: morphological dilation — grow Color outward by one layer
- gn_shrink_color: morphological erosion — remove the outer layer of Color
- gn_majority_nbr4: most frequent color among 4-neighbors
- gn_conway_step: one cellular automaton step — kill Color cells with exactly N Color neighbors
