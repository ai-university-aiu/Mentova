Mentova Accomplishment 174: The 2D Grid Extrema, Local Peaks, and Threshold Filtering Pack

PrologAI gains its 154th work package today.

The extrema pack (Layer 133, ex_* prefix) provides 14 predicates for finding
maximum and minimum values and their positions in 2D grids, detecting local
peaks and valleys, computing row and column argmax/argmin, and threshold
filtering.

The 14 predicates cover:

Global extrema: maximum and minimum values (ex_max_val, ex_min_val);
positions of all cells holding the maximum or minimum (ex_max_cells,
ex_min_cells).

Row and column argmax/argmin: 0-based row index with the maximum row sum
(ex_row_argmax); column index with the maximum column sum (ex_col_argmax);
symmetric argmin (ex_row_argmin, ex_col_argmin). First occurrence wins on ties.

Local peaks and valleys: cells strictly greater than all in-bounds
4-connected neighbors (ex_local_max4); cells strictly less than all
in-bounds neighbors (ex_local_min4).

Threshold filtering: cells with value above a threshold (ex_above); cells
below (ex_below).

Scalar summary: max minus min (ex_range); positions of all non-zero cells
(ex_nonzero).
