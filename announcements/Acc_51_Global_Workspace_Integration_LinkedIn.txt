WHAT WAS BUILT

The PrologAI Global Workspace Cycle (PR 18) and Attention Economy (PR 32) are
now integrated into Mentova.

The workspace cycle:
• Node_facts from Mentova's 48 reasoning rungs compete as candidate coalitions.
• Salience = novelty (40%) + goal relevance (30%) + affect (20%) - habituation.
• One winner per cycle. Broadcast on channel broadcast://APEX_MIND/cycle.
• SONA learning attaches to every broadcast.
• Habituation decays repeated winners. The spotlight does not perseverate.

The attention economy:
• Every node_fact now carries STI (short-term importance) and LTI (long-term importance).
• Winning a broadcast earns wages. Disuse costs rent.
• STI spreads to co-activated neighbors.
• Total currency is capped. Runaway attention feedback is structurally impossible.

WHY THIS MATTERS

Mentova was a reasoner. It could answer questions across 48 reasoning types with
glass-box justifications. But it answered one question at a time, on demand.

Now it has a cycle. On each beat, it selects what matters most, broadcasts it to
all 48 rungs simultaneously, and the attention economy records what was useful.

The workspace is the bridge between the reasoning ladder and self-improvement.
Facts that win broadcasts earn attention currency. Facts that are never broadcast
decay toward eviction. Over time, the knowledge store becomes shaped by what the
mind actually uses - not by what was originally loaded.

This is the minimal architecture for a mind that grows from experience.
