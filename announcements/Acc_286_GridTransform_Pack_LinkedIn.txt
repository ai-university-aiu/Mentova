Acc_286: PrologAI gains transformation detection and application between grids.

The gridtransform pack (Layer 245, gtr_* prefix) adds 14 predicates for detecting
and applying color transformations between pairs of grids.

gtr_color_map detects the consistent color mapping from grid A to grid B (fails if
any source color maps to two different destinations). gtr_apply_color_map applies
the map. gtr_invert_map inverts it. gtr_compose_maps chains two maps together.

Beyond color maps: gtr_diff_cells and gtr_diff_count find where two grids differ.
gtr_changed_colors records the old and new color at each changed position.
gtr_delta_grid produces a grid showing only the changes. gtr_overlay pastes a
delta grid onto a base. The two compose cleanly: overlay(A, delta(A,B,Bg), Bg) = B.

gtr_is_color_permutation tests that two grids are related by a bijective color map.
