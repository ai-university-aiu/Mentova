WHAT IS AN ATTENTION SCHEMA?

Attention schema theory (Michael Graziano, 2013) proposes that awareness is not
attention itself - it is the brain's simplified model of its own attention, used
to predict and steer the attentional spotlight.

When you know you are focusing on something, what you know is a model of your
attention, not the neurons firing. The model is slightly wrong. But it's useful.
It lets you control where your attention goes next.
