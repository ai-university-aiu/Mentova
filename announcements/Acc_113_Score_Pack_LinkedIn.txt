Mentova just gained its 91st work package: the score pack.

14 sc_* predicates for measuring how well a candidate grid matches an expected output:

  sc_accuracy     - pixel accuracy as a float (0.0 to 1.0)
  sc_color_recall - fraction of target-color cells correctly produced
  sc_color_precision - fraction of produced cells that are correct
  sc_color_f1     - harmonic mean of recall and precision
  sc_pairs_score  - mean accuracy over all training pairs
  sc_pairs_perfect - did we solve every training pair exactly?
  sc_rank         - sort candidates best-first
  sc_best         - pick the winner
  sc_threshold    - keep only candidates above a minimum accuracy

This completes the four-pack ARC-AGI-2 foundation layer:

  Obj pack     - extract objects from grids
  Pipeline pack - thread grids through named steps
  Context pack  - store and look up symbol tables
  Score pack    - evaluate and select the best hypothesis

The compositional loop is now complete:
extract -> symbolize -> apply -> score -> repeat if needed.
