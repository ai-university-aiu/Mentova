D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU, Creator of PrologAI and Mentova, ORCID 0009-0001-9246-5758, linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/

PrologAI just crossed 64 work packages.

The new color pack gives Mentova color as a first-class concept across an entire grid.

cl_palette - the sorted list of distinct colors in a grid.

cl_count - how many cells have a given color.

cl_histogram - a Color-Count pair for every palette color.

cl_dominant and cl_rarest - the most and least frequent colors.

cl_replace and cl_remap - replace one color or apply a full color mapping.

cl_swap - swap two colors simultaneously.

cl_isolate and cl_remove - filter a grid down to one color or remove a color entirely.

cl_same_palette - test whether two grids use the same set of colors.

cl_is_mono and cl_color_count - single-color test and palette size.

This matters because:

Two grids that look different may have the same palette - now Mentova can prove that in one call.

Finding the rarest color is a common step in visual induction - now Mentova has it as a primitive.

Swapping two colors is a common transformation rule - now Mentova can represent and apply it directly.

Color is the foundation. Everything else - shapes, borders, paths, symmetry - is color arranged in space.

The color pack is the 64th work package in PrologAI and Mentova's 86th accomplishment.
