I am pleased to announce the accomplishment of Rung 45 of the Mentova reasoning ladder: Motivational Reasoning.

Mentova can now identify the highest-priority drive motivating any agent, recommend the action it implies, and verify that lower-level needs are first satisfied.

Demonstrated live:

    mentova_query(motivational, top_drive(alice), R)
    → drive(alice, achieve_recognition, high)

    mentova_query(motivational, drive_to_action(achieve_recognition), R)
    → action(compete_and_excel)

    mentova_query(motivational, hierarchy(esteem), R)
    → must_first_satisfy([physiological, safety, belonging])

Every motivational answer traces to a drive strength and a Maslow-level precondition — so Mentova can explain not just what an agent will do, but why.
