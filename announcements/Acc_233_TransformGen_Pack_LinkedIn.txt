PrologAI now has a transformgen pack - 14 tg_* predicates that systematically
enumerate every plausible rule transformation candidate from scene data.

The key idea: instead of guessing the right rule, generate ALL rules
derivable from the scene within a bounded search space, then filter to
those consistent with every training example.

tg_recolor_candidates: all recolor(Old,New) for distinct color pairs.
tg_shift_candidates: all shift(DR,DC) within bounds (48 rules for MaxShift=3).
tg_color_map_candidate: derive a color_map rule from one training pair.
tg_from_pairs: union of all generators across all training pairs.
tg_filter_consistent: the filter step - keep only rules that explain every pair.
tg_all_scene_candidates: complete enumeration from a single scene.

This closes the coverage gap in the ARC-AGI-2 reasoning stack. Gridsolve
uses heuristic inference for speed. Transformgen ensures the right rule is
never missed for cases where heuristics fall short.

42 of 42 unit tests pass.
