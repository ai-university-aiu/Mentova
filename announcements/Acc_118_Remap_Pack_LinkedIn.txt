Accomplishment 118: The Remap Pack - Color Remapping and Palette Manipulation

Mentova's 96th work package is live.

The remap pack adds 14 rm_* predicates for value transformation in grids:
replacing, swapping, and mapping colors according to learned substitutions;
normalizing palettes; shifting and clamping numeric values; and binarizing
grids to foreground and background.

This closes the loop from rule discovery to rule application. Where the seek
pack answers "where does this pattern occur?" and the induct pack answers
"what rule applies here?", the remap pack answers "how do I apply the rule
to produce the output?"

All 38 acceptance tests pass. The pack is documented in SPARC Specification
v63 (Part 88, RM-RM-001 through RM-RM-014), Refinement v111, and the
tutorial Chapter 59.
