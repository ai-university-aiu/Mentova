PrologAI now has an objmerge pack - 14 mg_* predicates for combining, decomposing, and reshaping object terms at the cell-set level.

The pack provides union, intersection, difference, and symmetric difference of two obj cell sets. It concatenates objects horizontally or vertically with a configurable gap. It merges or subtracts entire lists of objects. It fills an object's bounding box, extracts just the outer frame, or adds a border of padding. And it splits a single object into its 4-connected or 8-connected components using BFS flood-fill.

41 of 41 unit tests pass.

This completes the object-level manipulation toolkit. A reasoner can now combine, split, frame, and pad symbolic objects directly - without converting to a grid. Together with objxf, objcopy, and objmorph, this is a full suite for structural object reasoning.
