Mentova Accomplishment 179: The Variance Statistics Pack

PrologAI gains its 159th work package today.

The varstat pack (Layer 138, vt_* prefix) provides 14 predicates for integer
summation, floor and rounded arithmetic means, per-element deviation from the
mean, and position analysis for cells above or below various means in 2D grids.

The 14 predicates cover:

List statistics: vt_sum (total), vt_mean_floor (floor mean), vt_mean_round
(rounded mean using banker's rounding), vt_deviation (signed per-element
deviation from floor mean), vt_abs_deviation (absolute deviation).

Per-row and per-column sums: vt_row_sums and vt_col_sums give the total
for each row or column as a list.

Per-row and per-column means: vt_row_means and vt_col_means give the floor
mean for each row or column.

Global analysis: vt_global_mean gives the floor mean of all cells in the
grid.

Position selection: vt_above_mean and vt_below_mean find cells above or
below the global mean. vt_row_above_mean finds cells above their row mean.
vt_col_above_mean finds cells above their column mean.

Example: for [[1,2,3],[4,5,6],[7,8,9]]:
vt_global_mean -> 5 (45 divided by 9 cells = 5)
vt_above_mean -> [1-2, 2-0, 2-1, 2-2] (values 6, 7, 8, 9)
vt_row_above_mean -> [0-2, 1-2, 2-2] (rightmost cell in each row)
vt_col_above_mean -> [2-0, 2-1, 2-2] (bottom cell in each column)
