Acc_284: PrologAI gains object grouping, filtering, sorting, pairing, and counting.

The gridgroupby pack (Layer 243, ggb_* prefix) adds 14 predicates that operate
on the ob(Color,Cells,BBox) object lists produced by the gridobj pack.

Group objects by color, size, row, or column. Filter to objects of a specific
color or size, or above/below a size threshold. Sort by size ascending or
descending, or by spatial position (row or column). Pair up objects that share
a color (exactly two per color). Count how many objects of each color exist.

All 14 predicates are pure list transformations - no grid access, fully composable.

This gives the platform the relational vocabulary needed to answer questions like:
"which color has exactly two objects?", "what are the three smallest objects?",
"group all objects by which row they start in".
