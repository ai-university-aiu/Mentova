Accomplishment 102: Mentova now has binary mask operations and grid noise analysis.

The noise pack (ns_* predicates, Layer 61) gives Mentova 14 new tools:

Binary masks:
- ns_mask_apply/4: replace masked cells with a fill color
- ns_mask_from_color/3: build a mask from a color criterion
- ns_mask_invert/3, ns_mask_and/3, ns_mask_or/3: compose masks
- ns_mask_to_region/2, ns_region_to_mask/4: bridge masks and region lists

Noise analysis:
- ns_majority_color/2: find the dominant color
- ns_noise_cells/3: find cells that don't match the majority
- ns_denoise/3: replace noise with the majority color
- ns_sparse_cells/3: find cells belonging to rare colors
- ns_dense_cells/3: find cells belonging to common colors
- ns_isolate_color/3: keep one color, zero out everything else

With this pack, Mentova can now form hypotheses like "the output is the input with noise removed" and verify them against real ARC-AGI grid pairs.
