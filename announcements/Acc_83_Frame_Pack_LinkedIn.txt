PrologAI just crossed 61 work packages.

The new frame pack gives Mentova the concept of a bordered structure.

A frame is a uniform-color outer ring surrounding an interior region.

fr_border_color gives you the border's color.

fr_inner strips the border and returns the interior.

fr_ring_count tells you how many concentric rings a grid has.

fr_bounding_box finds the outermost ring of a given color anywhere in the grid.

fr_is_nested detects two-layer nesting: outer ring of one color, second ring of another.

fr_make_framed builds a bordered grid from scratch. fr_add_border wraps an existing grid.

This matters because bordered structures appear constantly in visual reasoning tasks: colored rectangles with distinct interiors, nested containers, frames within frames.

Now Mentova can detect, measure, generate, and reason about them as named concepts - not just as patterns discovered by ad-hoc loops.

The frame pack is the 61st work package in PrologAI and Mentova's 83rd accomplishment.
