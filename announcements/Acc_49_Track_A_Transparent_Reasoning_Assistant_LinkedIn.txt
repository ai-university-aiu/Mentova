Mentova has passed Track A of its Demonstration Plan.

Track A is the Transparent Reasoning Assistant - no hardware, no neural network, no
opaque weights. Just a mind that answers expert questions and shows every step.

THE DEMONSTRATION

Mentova was asked ten expert biomedical questions over two real ontologies:

• the Gene Ontology (GO) - 43,000+ terms classifying what genes do and where
• the Disease Ontology (DO) - 10,000+ terms classifying human diseases

Each ontology was loaded into its own scope. Every answer came back with the full
chain of named facts that produced it.

SAMPLE RESULTS (all glass-box)

Is DNA repair a kind of biological process? Yes - via a four-hop is_a chain:
dna_repair -> dna_metabolic_process -> metabolic_process -> biological_process.
Every step is a named fact. Every fact is auditable.

Is protein_tyrosine_kinase_activity a kind of molecular_function? Yes - via a
five-hop chain through the GO molecular function hierarchy. Computed correctly
without a depth limit.

What diseases is TP53 associated with? Cancer, lung cancer, colorectal cancer,
and breast cancer - all curated associations, all named in the justification.

Is glioblastoma a kind of disease? Yes - via a five-hop chain:
glioblastoma -> brain_cancer -> cancer -> disease_of_cellular_proliferation -> disease.

CROSS-SCOPE RESULT

Explain breast cancer via the GO functions of its genes:
BRCA1 contributes dna_binding. TP53 contributes
dna_binding_transcription_factor_activity. PTEN contributes
protein_tyrosine_phosphatase_activity. One query. Two ontologies. Every link named.

WHY THIS MATTERS

Any black-box model can return "dna_binding" as an answer to "what does BRCA1 do."

What no black-box model can do is show you the specific node_facts that produced
the answer, the evidence code on each fact, and the exact chain of is_a links used
to classify it in the ontology.

Mentova does all three. Always. That is what glass-box reasoning means.

Track A is the practical proof that the 48-rung reasoning ladder is not just a
demonstration - it is a foundation for tools that domain experts can actually audit.
