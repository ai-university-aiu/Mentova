Accomplishment 120: The Window Pack - Sliding Window and Neighborhood Operations

Mentova's 98th work package is live.

The window pack adds 14 wn_* predicates for neighborhood operations: 4-connected
and 8-connected neighbor enumeration, neighbor counting, sub-grid extraction,
sliding windows, grid padding, local maxima and minima detection, halo
computation, convolution, and spatial distance operations.

These are the primitives that make local spatial reasoning possible. With the
window pack, Mentova can find which cells border a region, detect structural
peaks, slide a template across a grid, and measure spatial distances.

All 42 acceptance tests pass. The pack is documented in SPARC Specification
v65 (Part 90, WN-WN-001 through WN-WN-014), Refinement v113, and the
tutorial Chapter 61.
