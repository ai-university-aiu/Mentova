Accomplishment 87 - Mentova now reasons about shapes independent of position and color.

D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU, Creator of PrologAI and Mentova, ORCID 0009-0001-9246-5758, linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/

PrologAI just crossed 65 work packages.

The new shape pack gives Mentova the concept of a spatial pattern separated from where it is and what color it is.

sh_from_cells - normalize any list of grid cells to a canonical position-independent shape.

sh_from_grid - extract all cells of a given color from a grid and immediately normalize.

sh_area and sh_bounding_size - how many cells and what bounding box.

sh_contains_cell and sh_equal - membership and identity.

sh_rotate90, sh_reflect_h, sh_reflect_v - the three generators of the D4 dihedral group on a shape.

sh_orbit - all distinct orientations of a shape under D4 (1 to 8 elements).

sh_canonical - the lex-min D4 orientation; the fingerprint of a shape class.

sh_equivalent - two shapes are equivalent if they belong to the same D4 orbit.

sh_translate and sh_to_grid - place a shape at any offset with any color.

This matters because:

An L-shape and its rotation are the same shape. Now Mentova can prove that with one call.

Two different colors in the same grid might encode the same shape. Now Mentova can detect that.

Finding the orientation of a pattern that matches a target is now a loop over sh_orbit. No special cases.

The grid pack gives cells. The color pack gives palette. The shape pack gives patterns. These three together are the perceptual foundation.

The shape pack is the 65th work package in PrologAI and Mentova's 87th accomplishment.
