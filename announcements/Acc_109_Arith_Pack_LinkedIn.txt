Accomplishment 109: Mentova gains cell-wise arithmetic on grids - a 14-predicate numerical reasoning layer for adding, subtracting, multiplying, summing, and clamping grid values.

The arith pack (ar_* predicates, Layer 68) gives Mentova 14 new tools for arithmetic over list-of-lists grids:

- ar_cell_add/3, ar_cell_sub/3, ar_cell_mul/3: cell-wise binary grid operations
- ar_cell_abs_diff/3: cell-wise absolute difference (always non-negative)
- ar_cell_mod/3: apply modulo N to every cell
- ar_scalar_add/3, ar_scalar_mul/3: shift or scale every cell by a constant
- ar_cell_clamp/4: clamp every cell to a range [Lo, Hi]
- ar_row_sum/3, ar_col_sum/3: sum one row or column
- ar_row_sums/2, ar_col_sums/2: sum every row or every column
- ar_cell_max/2, ar_cell_min/2: grid-wide maximum and minimum

With arithmetic, Mentova can now measure how much a grid changed between two states, normalize values to a valid color range, compare grids by their row and column weight distributions, and apply scaling as part of a visual reasoning pipeline.
