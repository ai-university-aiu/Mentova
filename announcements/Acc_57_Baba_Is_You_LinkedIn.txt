MENTOVA SOLVES BABA IS YOU — THE JEWEL

Baba Is You is a puzzle game with one rule: the rules of the game are objects
in the game world. They are written as word-blocks. You can push them.
When they move, the rules change.

Push STOP away from WALL IS [STOP], and walls are no longer stop.
Walk through what was a wall to reach the flag. Win.

The PrologAI Demonstration Plan calls this "the jewel" - and here is why:

In Baba Is You, rules are word-block positions in a grid.
They are named. They are readable. They can be changed by moving a block.

In PrologAI, rules are node_facts in the lattice nexus.
They are named. They are readable. They can be changed with assert/retract.

The mechanics are structurally identical.

Today Mentova solves a Baba Is You puzzle glass-box (Accomplishment Record Acc_57).

The puzzle (6x5 grid):

    Col: 0    1    2    3    4
    Row 0: .    .   WAL   .    .
    Row 1: BAB  .   WAL   .   FLA
    Row 2: .    .   WAL   .    .
    Row 3: wBA  wIS  wYO   .    .
    Row 4: wFL  wIS  wWI   .    .
    Row 5: wWA  wIS  wST   .    .

Active rules (parsed from word-block positions):
- BABA IS YOU: Baba at (1,0) is the player.
- FLAG IS WIN: touching flag at (1,4) wins.
- WALL IS STOP: walls at column 2 block movement.

Problem: Baba cannot cross the column-2 wall barrier to reach the flag.

Mentova's five steps, glass-box:

Step 1 - Parse rules. Scan word-block positions. Three NOUN IS PROP chains found.
AC-PR57-001: PASS — BABA IS YOU, FLAG IS WIN, WALL IS STOP all parsed.

Step 2 - Identify blocker. WALL IS STOP is active. Column-2 walls have STOP.
Baba at (1,0) cannot reach flag at (1,4).
AC-PR57-002: PASS — blocking rule: WALL IS STOP.

Step 3 - Reason about the rule-breaking push. WALL IS STOP chain:
word(wall)(5,0) - word(is)(5,1) - word(stop)(5,2).
Push word(stop) from (5,2) to (5,3). IS and STOP become non-adjacent. Chain breaks.
AC-PR57-003: PASS — push: move word(stop) one column right.

Step 4 - Apply push. Re-parse rules.
After push: only BABA IS YOU and FLAG IS WIN remain. WALL IS STOP: absent.
AC-PR57-004: PASS — WALL IS STOP removed.

Step 5 - Find winning path.
(1,1) -> (1,2) [through wall - no STOP] -> (1,3) -> (1,4) [FLAG - WIN!]
AC-PR57-005: PASS — winning path found and printed glass-box.

The PrologAI parallel, printed at the end:
- Baba Is You: push word(stop) block -> WALL IS STOP retracted.
- PrologAI:    retract(lattice_node_fact(_, _, wall_is_stop, _, _)).
- Baba Is You: BABA IS YOU governs which tile the player controls.
- PrologAI:    node_fact(baba, is, you) -> agent identity node_fact.
- The mechanic is identical: rules are named, readable, writable facts.

This is why the Demonstration Plan calls Baba Is You "the jewel."
Not because it is the hardest. Because it is the most fitting.
A puzzle whose mechanic IS the mechanic of the platform solving it.
