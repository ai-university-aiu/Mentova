Mentova just gained its 93rd work package: the hyp pack.

14 hy_* predicates for applying, testing, and selecting rule hypotheses:

  hy_color_sub      - apply a color substitution map (identity fallback)
  hy_identity       - no-op hypothesis
  hy_test_all       - mean pixel accuracy over all training pairs
  hy_verify_all     - did we solve every training pair exactly?
  hy_rank           - rank hypotheses by accuracy
  hy_select         - pick the winner
  hy_compose        - chain two color maps
  hy_invert_map     - reverse the substitution

The observation-hypothesis-test loop is now complete:

1. Observe: in_color_map_pairs extracts the color rule from training pairs
2. Hypothesize: hy_from_map([1-5]) builds the callable
3. Test: hy_test_all measures mean accuracy
4. Verify: hy_verify_all confirms all pairs solved
5. Apply: submit the answer

This is a full induction pipeline for color substitution rules.
