Accomplishment 122: The Tile Pack - Tiling, Stamping, and Period Detection

Mentova's 100th work package is live.

The tile pack adds 14 ti_* predicates for tiling operations on grids:
tiling a motif horizontally and vertically, creating NR-by-NC arrangements
of copies, splitting grids into bands and stripes, reassembling tiles back
into one grid, stamping a motif at any position (or multiple positions),
extracting named tile positions, checking if a grid is an exact tiling of
one motif, finding the smallest horizontal and vertical periods, and
generating checkerboard patterns.

These primitives answer the pattern questions that visual reasoning requires:
what motif tiles this grid? how often does this pattern repeat? where do I
stamp this shape?

All 42 acceptance tests pass. The pack is documented in SPARC Specification
v67 (Part 92, TI-TI-001 through TI-TI-014), Refinement v115, and tutorial
Chapter 63.
