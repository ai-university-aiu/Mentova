PrologAI now has a colortable pack - 14 ct_* predicates that learn a complete
color substitution table from Before-After scene pair training examples.

The core question: what color becomes what? If red becomes green and blue
becomes yellow across every training example, then that is the rule. Apply
it to the test input and you are done.

ct_learn_map processes a list of Before-After pairs and accumulates a consistent
color map. Conflicting pairs (same source color, different target) are returned
separately rather than corrupting the map.

ct_apply_to_scene applies the learned table to any scene. Colors not in the
table are left unchanged. ct_complete_map can fill in identity entries for
any uncovered colors. ct_invert_map reverses the table for the inverse task.

Positional matching: objects are paired by position (first with first, second
with second), which is correct for ARC-AGI-2 scenes where spatial structure
is preserved and only colors change.
