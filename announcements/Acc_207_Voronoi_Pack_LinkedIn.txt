Mentova Accomplishment 207: Nearest-Color Painting and Voronoi Partitioning

PrologAI now includes a voronoi pack (vn_*, Layer 166) - 14 predicates for
painting background cells with the nearest non-background color.

The concept: in many visual reasoning tasks, you see a grid with colored objects
and empty space. You need to fill the empty space with the color of the nearest
object. That's Voronoi coloring. Now it's one predicate call:

    vn_paint_bg(Grid, Bg, Painted).

The pack also provides:

- vn_nearest_dist: Manhattan distance from any cell to the nearest non-background cell
- vn_nearest_color: the color of the nearest non-background cell
- vn_dist_map: the full distance transform (each cell -> its distance to nearest non-Bg)
- vn_region_cells / vn_regions: which background cells belong to which color's region
- vn_max_dist / vn_at_dist / vn_within_dist: distance-based filtering
- vn_medial: the Voronoi edge cells - background cells equidistant from two+ colors
- vn_expand1 / vn_expand_n: grow a color region N steps into the background
