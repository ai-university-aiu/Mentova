Accomplishment 125: The Morph Pack - Morphological Grid Operations

Mentova's 103rd work package is live.

The morph pack adds 14 mo_* predicates for morphological grid operations:
expanding all non-background regions by one 4-connected step with the
neighbor's color (mo_dilate), shrinking regions by one step (mo_erode),
applying dilation or erosion N times (mo_dilate_n, mo_erode_n), morphological
open/close/smooth compound operations, extracting only perimeter cells
(mo_boundary) or only interior cells (mo_interior), dilating with a fixed
fill value (mo_dilate_val), BFS flooding from seed cells into background
territory (mo_grow_from), computing the L1 Manhattan distance from each
foreground cell to the nearest background cell (mo_dist_to_bg), extracting
the shell of cells added at exactly the Nth dilation step (mo_ring), and
filling all enclosed background regions not reachable from the grid border
(mo_fill_holes).

These primitives give Mentova a complete morphological toolkit: it can
expand and contract objects, detect their boundaries, measure how deep
interior cells lie from the surface, grow regions outward from seeds,
and identify holes enclosed within object boundaries.

All 42 acceptance tests pass. The pack is documented in SPARC Specification
v70 (Part 95, MO-MO-001 through MO-MO-014), Refinement v118, and tutorial
Chapter 66.
