Acc_285: PrologAI gains spatial reasoning between objects.

The gridrelation pack (Layer 244, grl_* prefix) adds 14 predicates for computing
spatial relations between the ob/3 object terms produced by the gridobj pack.

Is object A touching object B? Are they adjacent within N cells? What is the
minimum distance? Is A entirely above B? Does A's bbox contain B's bbox? Do they
overlap at the cell level? Do they share any rows or columns? What direction would
you travel from A to B?

The direction predicate uses doubled bbox centroids for exact integer arithmetic
with no floating point. The all-relations predicate returns every true relation as
a list: touching, above, below, left_of, right_of, bbox_contains, bbox_overlap,
cells_overlap, same_rows, same_cols.

With gridobj (identify objects), gridgroupby (group and filter), and gridrelation
(spatial relations), the platform now has a complete three-level object reasoning
pipeline.
