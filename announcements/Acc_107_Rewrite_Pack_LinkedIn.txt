Accomplishment 107: Mentova gains rule-based grid cell rewriting - color maps, region paint, mask, overlay, stamp, diff, normalize, invert, and conditional recolor.

The rewrite pack (rw_* predicates, Layer 66) gives Mentova 14 new tools for transforming the color values of visual grids:

- rw_map_color/3: apply a substitution map (list of Old-New color pairs) to every cell
- rw_replace_color/4, rw_swap_colors/4: one-color and two-color rewriting shortcuts
- rw_set_region/4, rw_fill_rect/7, rw_set_border/3: paint a region, rectangle, or outermost ring
- rw_mask_apply/5: use a binary mask to select which cells receive a fill color
- rw_overlay/4: composite two grids - non-background cells of Grid2 overwrite Grid1
- rw_stamp/5: paste a patch grid at a given offset
- rw_diff_apply/3: apply a list of r(R,C)-Color cell edits
- rw_normalize/3: renumber distinct non-background colors to 1, 2, 3, ...
- rw_invert_colors/3, rw_remap_bg/4: complement colors or change background
- rw_conditional/5: per-cell conditional recolor based on a goal predicate

Combined with the connect pack (find objects), the morph pack (reshape objects), and the induction pack (detect patterns), the rewrite pack closes the loop: Mentova can now perceive structure, understand transformation patterns, and actively rewrite grids according to learned rules.
