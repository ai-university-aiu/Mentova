MENTOVA FOLLOWS A STORY'S CAUSAL THREAD — Rung 41 Accomplished

Author: D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU (Artificial Intelligence University). Creator of PrologAI and Mentova. Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCID): 0009-0001-9246-5758. (https://www.linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/)


I am pleased to announce the accomplishment of Rung 41 of the Mentova reasoning ladder: Narrative Reasoning.

Mentova can now extract the causal-temporal structure of a story: setup, conflict, resolution, character roles, and the causal chain between events.

Demonstrated live:

    mentova_query(narrative, arc(tortoise_hare), R)
    → arc(setup(hare_boasts), conflict(race_begins), resolution(tortoise_wins))

    mentova_query(narrative, causal_chain(boy_wolf), R)
    → chain: boy_lies_wolf → villagers_respond → boy_lies_again → villagers_ignore → real_wolf_comes → boy_cries_for_help → villagers_ignore → wolf_eats_sheep

    mentova_query(narrative, role(tortoise_hare, tortoise), R)
    → protagonist (slow but steady)

Every story element — who, what, why, when — is traceable to a structured narrative fact.
