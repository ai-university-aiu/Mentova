PrologAI can now shift and roll grids in any direction.

Work Package 133 - the offset pack - adds 14 od_* predicates for grid shifting and rolling. 42/42 tests.

Two operations, four directions each:

Shifting: content moves; vacated cells become background; content that falls off an edge is dropped.
Rolling: content wraps around; no content is lost.

od_shift_right / od_shift_left / od_shift_down / od_shift_up
od_roll_right / od_roll_left / od_roll_down / od_roll_up

Plus:
- od_shift_color: move only one specific color, leave everything else unchanged
- od_infer_shift: given two grids, find (DR, DC) such that shifting one gives the other

od_shift_dir dispatches by direction atom. It uses if-then-else rather than separate clauses - this matters for determinism: multi-clause dispatch leaves choicepoints for the unmatched directions.
