MENTOVA UNDERSTANDS ROLES AND RELATIONSHIPS — Rung 42 Accomplished

Author: D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU (Artificial Intelligence University). Creator of PrologAI and Mentova. Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCID): 0009-0001-9246-5758. (https://www.linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/)


I am pleased to announce the accomplishment of Rung 42 of the Mentova reasoning ladder: Social Reasoning.

Mentova can now reason about social roles, relationships, and norms — predicting contextually appropriate behaviour from the social structure of a situation.

Demonstrated live:

    mentova_query(social, role(alice, school), R)
    → role(alice, teacher, school)

    mentova_query(social, relationship(alice, bob), R)
    → teacher_student, strength(0.8)

    mentova_query(social, norms(teacher, school), R)
    → [prepare_lessons(obligatory), treat_fairly(obligatory), grade_objectively(obligatory)]

Every answer includes the role, context, and applicable norms — not just a label.
