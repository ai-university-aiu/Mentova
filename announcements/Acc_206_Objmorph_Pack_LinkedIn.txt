Mentova Accomplishment 206: Morphological Operations on Object Terms

PrologAI now includes an objmorph pack (om_*, Layer 165) - 14 predicates for
binary morphology directly on obj(Color, Cells) terms.

What does that mean? Binary morphology is the science of analyzing the shape
and boundary of discrete regions. Operations like "find the outer edge,"
"shrink the region by one step," "expand the region by one step," "remove thin
protrusions" - all fundamental tools for visual reasoning.

The new pack provides:

- om_neighbors4 and om_neighbors8: the 4 or 8 cells surrounding any cell
- om_boundary4 / om_interior4: outer ring and inner core under 4-connectivity
- om_boundary8 / om_interior8: outer ring and inner core under 8-connectivity
- om_dilate4 / om_dilate8: expand the cell set by one step
- om_erode4 / om_erode8: shrink the cell set by one step
- om_dilate4_n / om_erode4_n: repeat N times
- om_open4: erode then dilate (cleans up thin appendages)
- om_close4: dilate then erode (fills small gaps)

All 14 predicates work directly on obj terms - no grid conversion required.
