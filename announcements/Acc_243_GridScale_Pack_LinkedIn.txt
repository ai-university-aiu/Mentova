D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU, Creator and Owner of PrologAI and Mentova, ORCID 0009-0001-9246-5758, linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/

PrologAI Work Package 223 is merged: the gridscale pack (gsc_*, Layer 202) adds
fourteen predicates for integer block-pixel scaling of raw grids.

The pack upsamples grids by repeating each cell into NxN blocks, downsamples
by collapsing uniform blocks, detects the scale factor between two grids, infers
the smallest repeated tile, and also supports majority-vote downscaling,
nearest-neighbor resize, stride subsampling, border padding, and factor-aligned
cropping.

Key predicates:
- gsc_upsample / gsc_downsample: exact integer scaling (downsample fails on non-uniform blocks)
- gsc_block_majority: majority-vote downscaling (never fails)
- gsc_scale_factor / gsc_is_scale_of: detect and verify scaling relationships
- gsc_infer_tile: find the smallest tile whose upsampling equals a given grid
- gsc_factors: list all valid block sizes for a grid
- gsc_resize: nearest-neighbor resize to arbitrary target dimensions
- gsc_pad / gsc_crop_to_factor: border padding and factor-aligned cropping
