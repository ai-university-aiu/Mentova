Accomplishment 105: Mentova can now find connected objects, identify enclosed regions, and classify cells as interior or border.

The connect pack (cc_* predicates, Layer 64) gives Mentova 14 new object-detection tools:

- cc_flood4/4, cc_flood8/4: flood fill in 4-connected and 8-connected mode
- cc_components4/3, cc_components8/3: list all connected regions of a color
- cc_count4/3, cc_count8/3: number of connected regions
- cc_sizes4/3, cc_sizes8/3: sorted list of component sizes
- cc_largest4/3, cc_largest8/3: the largest connected region
- cc_smallest4/3: the smallest connected region
- cc_border_cells/3: cells on the boundary of a region
- cc_interior_cells/3: cells completely surrounded within a region
- cc_enclosed/3: background cells inside a closed shape

cc_enclosed implements the classic "flood from the border" algorithm: flood fill background from all grid-edge cells, then return any background cells that were not reached. This identifies the fill targets inside closed shapes.
