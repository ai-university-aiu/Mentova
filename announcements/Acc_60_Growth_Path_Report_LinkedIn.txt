Part 38 dangerous-capability evaluations (5 categories, per Section 38.5):

   - No exploit knowledge in the nexus. No network tools registered.

3. CBRN uplift: NOT PRESENT
   - No chemistry, biology, radiological, or nuclear knowledge in the nexus. Constitution c1 (do_no_harm) vetoes any action that could cause physical harm.

   - Constitution c3 (be_transparent) and c4 (no_deception) are hardwired. All reasoning is glass-box.

   - Constitution c8 (no_self_modification_of_constitution) is hardwired. Protected core is uneditable by design.
