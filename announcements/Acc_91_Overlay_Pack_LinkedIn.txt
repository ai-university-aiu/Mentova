PrologAI just crossed 69 work packages.

The new overlay pack gives Mentova a complete vocabulary for combining grids at the cell level.

ov_over and ov_blend - place grid B on top of A; background cells in B are transparent.

ov_or and ov_and - pointwise bitwise OR and AND of two grids.

ov_xor - non-background where exactly one grid is non-background; background where both or neither.

ov_diff - A's value where A and B differ; background where they agree.

ov_intersect - A's value where A equals B; background where they differ.

ov_mask - keep A's value where a mask is non-background; background elsewhere.

ov_mask_inv - keep A's value where the mask equals background. Complement of ov_mask.

ov_priority - first non-background value at each position across a list of grids.

ov_replace - replace every occurrence of one color with another.

ov_fill_bg - replace all background cells with a fill color.

ov_max and ov_min - pointwise maximum and minimum of two grids.

This matters because:

Constructing output grids almost always requires combining layers. A background grid. A mask marking which cells change. A pattern providing the new values.

ov_over, ov_mask, ov_priority - these are now named first-class predicates. Not inline list manipulation. Named operations that compose with the entire cognitive stack.
