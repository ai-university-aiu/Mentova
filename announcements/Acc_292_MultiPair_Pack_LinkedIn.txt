To understand an ARC task, you need to track the same object across all training examples, not just within one.

Work Package 272 introduces the multipair pack: 14 mp_* predicates for cross-pair object identity. tracked(Idx, Color, PairIdxList) gives every object a consistent identity. Universal vs variable colors separate content from markers. Disappeared and appeared objects flag what the task erases or creates.

273 work packages. Glass-box tracking from first principles.
