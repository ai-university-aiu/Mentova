14 predicates now answer collection-level questions about rows and columns

rs_col_at and rs_all_cols extract individual or all column lists
(top-to-bottom order).

rs_row_freq and rs_col_freq compute frequency tables: which rows (or
column lists) appear most often, and how many times.

rs_modal_row and rs_modal_col identify the most frequently occurring
row or column. Ties broken by largest value in standard order.

rs_uniq_rows and rs_uniq_cols find the rows and columns that appear
exactly once - the singletons, the odd-ones-out.

rs_dup_row_pairs and rs_dup_col_pairs find all index pairs where two
rows (or two columns) are identical.

rs_row_palindrome and rs_col_palindrome test whether a row or column
reads the same forward as backward.

rs_rows_anagram and rs_cols_anagram test whether two rows (or two
columns) have the same multiset of values - the same elements in any
order, with multiplicity preserved.
