Mentova just gained its 92nd work package: the induct pack.

14 in_* predicates for extracting the structure of change from training pairs:

  in_delta           - which cells changed and how (Old->New triples)
  in_color_map       - what color substitution rule applies (fails on conflict)
  in_color_map_pairs - the substitution consistent across ALL training pairs
  in_size_change     - did the grid grow or shrink?
  in_consistent_delta - is the same pattern in every training pair?
  in_bg_color        - what is the background color?
  in_invariant_cells - what stayed the same?

Before a system can hypothesize a rule, it must observe the evidence.

This is the observation layer for ARC-AGI-2 rule induction.

The full reasoning loop is now supported:
observe -> extract objects -> build symbol table -> apply -> score -> repeat
