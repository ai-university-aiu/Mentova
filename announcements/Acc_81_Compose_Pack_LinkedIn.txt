Accomplishment 81 - Mentova now composes transformation rules into pipelines.

D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU, Creator and Owner of PrologAI and Mentova, ORCID 0009-0001-9246-5758, linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/

PrologAI just crossed 59 work packages.

The new compose pack gives Mentova a meta-language for grid transformation.

Instead of writing a new predicate every time two operations need to be chained together, Mentova can now express the combination directly as a term - and apply it to any grid.

cp_pipe(Rule1, Rule2, Grid, Grid2) applies two rules in sequence.

cp_branch(Cond, ThenRule, ElseRule, Grid, Grid2) dispatches on grid content.

cp_fixed_point(Rule, Grid, Grid2) applies a rule until the grid stops changing.

cp_map_rows and cp_map_cols apply row-level or column-level rules to every row or column independently.

cp_fold reduces a list of grids to one using an accumulating rule.

This is 13 combinators total - and they compose with every other pack.

A motion predicate is a valid compose rule. A pattern predicate is a valid compose rule. A grid operation is a valid compose rule. The boundary between packs disappears at the pipeline level.

The compose pack is the 59th work package in PrologAI and Mentova's 81st accomplishment.
