D. R. Dison, Founder of AIU, Creator and Owner of PrologAI and Mentova, ORCID 0009-0001-9246-5758, linkedin.com/in/d-r-dison/

PrologAI Work Package 225 is merged: the gridflood pack (gf_*, Layer 204) adds
fourteen predicates for flood-fill, connected-region analysis, hole detection,
and connected-component enumeration on raw grids.

The pack seeds BFS from any cell to fill 4-connected or 8-connected same-color
regions, detects enclosed background holes unreachable from the grid border,
fills those holes, enumerates all connected components of a given color, and
returns the largest region. All 54 acceptance tests pass.

Key predicates:
- gf_fill / gf_fill8: 4-connected and 8-connected BFS flood-fill from seed cell
- gf_recolor: global palette swap regardless of connectivity
- gf_isolate: extract one region onto a blank background
- gf_region_cells / gf_region_size / gf_region_bbox: region geometry
- gf_enclosed_cells / gf_fill_enclosed: hole detection and filling
- gf_components / gf_n_components / gf_largest: connected-component enumeration
- gf_is_connected: connectivity test for 4 or 8 neighborhoods
- gf_boundary_fill: flood-fill stopping at a specified boundary color

All 54 acceptance tests pass. PrologAI now comprises 225 work packages
