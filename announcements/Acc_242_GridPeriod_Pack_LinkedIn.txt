PrologAI Work Package 222 is merged: the gridperiod pack (gper_*, Layer 201) adds
fourteen predicates for detecting and exploiting periodic structure in raw grids.

The pack finds the smallest repeating period along rows and columns, extracts the
fundamental tile, tiles small grids to larger target sizes, measures autocorrelation,
and applies wrap-around cyclic shifts.

Key predicates:
- gper_row_period / gper_col_period: find the minimum period in each dimension
- gper_tile_dims / gper_extract_tile: identify and recover the fundamental tile
- gper_tile_grid / gper_extend: reconstruct or grow grids by repeating the tile
- gper_autocorr_row / gper_autocorr_col: quantify periodic strength (0.0 to 1.0)
- gper_shift_wrap_rows / gper_shift_wrap_cols: cyclic row and column shifts
